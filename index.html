Pomegranate


Tony Moly Im Real Pomegranate Mask Sheet was the third one I decided to try. It is supposed to help you to restore skin elasticity. At the age of 25 it may not be the main issue and I may not see some visible results immediately but I think it is still a good mask to firm my skin a little bit.


Red Wine


Tony Moly Im Real Red Wine Mask Sheet is designed to take care of your pores. It helps to tighten uneven skin texture with enlarged pores. I do not suffer from enlarged pores, but I have a small amount of visible pores on my cheeks and I wonder how this mask will deal with them.


Hyaluronic Multi Care


Enesti Hyaluronic Multi Care Mask is a uniquely created mask that is based upon the water from the Suanbo spring in Korea. This water is stated to be rich with calcium, sodium, magnesium and zinc that are supposed to be good for your skin. Moreover, this mask also has hyaluronic acid among its ingredients to intensely moisturize your skin.


Collagen Multi Care


Enesti Collagen Multi Care Mask is another mask from the brand. It is based on the same type of water and also has the hydrolyzed collagen in its formulation.It allows you to get naturally radiant and smooth skin. Collagen helps your skin stay hydrated, revives your skin and adds radiance. I really wonder how this will work for me.



Collagen Voluming Serum

It’s Skin Collagen Voluming Serum is said to have hydrolyzed collagen in it that provides elasticity and nutrition to the skin. It allows you to get naturally smooth skin when used and suits every skin type. It’s Skin has a huge Collagen line and I decided to start with the serum as it is a very concentrated product that allows you to see results pretty quickly.
